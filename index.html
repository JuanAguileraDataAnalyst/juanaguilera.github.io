About Me

Database Analyst specializing in the design, development, and administration of relational database systems within integrated ERP, CRM, and e-commerce environments. My work focuses on building scalable data infrastructures that allow organizations to manage, integrate, and analyze operational data efficiently.


I design and maintain relational database architectures, develop automated ETL pipelines, and implement structured multi-source data integrations that support operational reporting and business intelligence. My experience includes extracting and transforming structured data through REST APIs and integrating systems such as ERP platforms, CRM tools, and e-commerce databases.


Currently working in Montréal, I contribute to building centralized database systems that consolidate product, inventory, sales, and customer data into unified data infrastructures used for analytics and decision-making.


My technical work includes SQL querying, Python scripting, Power Query (M) data transformation, and VBA automation to create reliable data pipelines and scalable reporting systems. I also implement data validation rules, governance standards, and database performance monitoring to ensure data integrity and system reliability.


I am passionate about designing structured data systems that transform complex operational data into actionable insights that support business growth and efficient decision-making.
